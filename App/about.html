About

Welcome to Mount Prayer Chain!

My name is Chance White. I am a Student at Mount St. Mary's university obtaining a degree in computer science. This project spawned from an idea that I and the University Chaplain had to give a platform to more students who are in need of prayers

There are 4 pages on this website. The home page is where users can view prayers from others users. if you want, you can like some of the prayers and they will be sent to your profile page so you can save them and pray for their intentions. There is also the post a prayer page where you, as a user can post your own prayer intention to your desired specifications. The profile page is where you can view intentions that you have post and delete some if you are desire and where you can view intentions that you have liked so you remember to pray for their intentions. The about page is where you are now! It has all the information that you may need to naviagte this site. Enjoy! God Bless!
